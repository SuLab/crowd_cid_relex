Title: Determine whether a chemical contributes to a disease

Overview

Scientists and doctors need to know whether a chemical (such as Aspirin) can potentially cause any diseases before they can give it to people. In this task, you will determine whether a sentence says a chemical contributes to a disease.

The Task

  1. One chemical and one disease have been identified and highlighted in a sentence. Sometimes a drug or disease may have multiple names (e.g., rapamycin and sirolimus). Assume that anything highlighted with the same colour is the same thing. When there are multiple names, all the names will be given in the answer choice.
  2. Read the sentence and determine whether it says that the chemical is contributing to or causing the disease (for example, as a side effect). Choose one of the three possible choices:
    • [chemical] directly contributes to or causes [disease]
      Select this choice when the sentence says that the chemical (by itself or with other chemicals) directly contributes to or causes the highlighted disease.
    • [chemical] contributes to another disease, and that other disease causes [disease]
      Select this choice when the chemical does not cause the disease directly, but rather the chemical causes another disease, and that other disease causes the highlighted disease.
    • The sentence does not say that [chemical] contributes to or causes [disease].
      Select this choice when the sentence does not say that the chemical contributes to the disease.
  3. Rate how confident you are in your answer. Any choice is ok.

Examples

  1. Feverolinus, a chemotherapy drug derived from mushrooms and used for treating breast cancer, has multiple side effects, including diarrhea, constipation, and hair loss.
    Feverolinus is a chemotherapy drug that has multiple side effects, including hair loss. The correct choice is:
    Feverolinus directly contributes to or causes hair loss
  2. Some of the patients, especially those over 50 years old, complained of experiencing chest aches and lower back pain two hours after taking adalamin and maximidin.
    Some of the patients developed chest aches after taking two drugs: adalamin and maximidin. Since the sentence doesn't say that the chest aches arose due to the lower back pain, the correct answer is:
    maximidin directly contributes to or causes chest aches
    Remember that if a drug is working in combination with another drug, then you should choose that it is contributing to the disease!
  3. Heart attacks are a well known downstream effect of barbatimol-induced artherosclerosis.
    Artherosclerosis (plaque buildup in arteries) causes heart attacks by restricting blood flow. Since barbatimol is inducing (causing) artherosclerosis, it is not directly contributing to heart attacks, but is still playing some role. Therefore the correct choice is:
    barbatimol contributes to another disease, and that other disease causes heart attacks
  4. The Food and Drug Administration has concluded its review of Pfizer's Lipitor and deemed it safe and efficacious for lowering high cholesterol.
    The sentence says that Lipitor lowers high cholesterol, which means it is curing the disease. The correct choice is:
    The sentence does not say that Lipitor contributes to or causes high cholesterol
